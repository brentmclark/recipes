# Smoked Meatloaf 
(A.K.A. "Mom, the Meatloaf!!!)

### Timing
- Prep Time :: 15 minutes
- Cook Time :: 4 hours

### Servings
4 to 6

### Ingredients
- 1 Cup breadcrumbs
- 1 Cup milk
- 2 Tablespoon onion, chopped
- 1/2 Teaspoon ground sage
- 2 Teaspoon salt
- 2 egg, beaten
- 2 Pound ground beef
- 1/4 Pound ground sausage
 
### Directions
1. Transfer the meatloaf to a wire rack and place on the grill
